Why Choose a Podiatrist?

A podiatrist is a medical specialist trained to diagnose and treat issues related to the feet, ankles, and lower limbs. Unlike general practitioners, podiatrists undergo specialized education and clinical training that enables them to handle complex foot-related health concerns. Conditions such as ingrown toenail removal, bunion surgery, sports injuries, diabetic foot issues, and heel pain all fall within their expertise.
